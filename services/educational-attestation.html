Educational Document Attestation

Ensuring your academic credentials are verified and recognized globally for study, work, or migration.

What is Educational Document Attestation?

Educational Document Attestation is the crucial process of verifying the authenticity of your academic documents (like degrees, diplomas, mark sheets, and transcripts) by designated authorities. This validation is often required when you plan to use these documents internationally for purposes such as higher education admission, employment applications, visa processing, or professional licensing.

The process typically involves multiple levels of verification, starting from state-level authorities (like HRD - Human Resource Department) to national-level (like MEA - Ministry of External Affairs) and finally, the embassy or consulate of the destination country.

Required Documents

While specific requirements can vary based on the document type and destination country, common documents include:

  • Original Degree / Diploma / Certificate
  • Original Mark Sheets / Transcripts (All years/semesters)
  • Passport Copy (Front and Back)
  • Authorization Letter (If applicable)
  • Recent Passport-sized Photographs
