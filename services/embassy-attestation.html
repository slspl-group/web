Embassy Attestation Service

Secure international document verification through official embassy channels for global recognition.

What is Embassy Attestation?

Embassy Attestation is a process of legalizing documents for use in foreign countries. It involves verifying the authenticity of documents by the embassy or consulate of the country where the documents will be used.

Process of Embassy Attestation

  1. Gather the required documents.
  2. Submit the documents to the relevant authorities for verification.
  3. Receive the attested documents from the embassy or consulate.
