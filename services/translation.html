Professional Translation Services

Accurate and certified translations for legal, personal, and business documents across multiple languages.

Bridging Language Barriers

In an increasingly globalized world, accurate communication is paramount. Saurabh Legal Services Pvt. Ltd. provides high-quality, professional translation services to ensure your documents are understood correctly, regardless of the language. Our services are often essential when submitting documents for attestation, visa applications, legal proceedings, or international business.

We work with a network of experienced, native-speaking translators specializing in various fields to deliver translations that are not only linguistically accurate but also culturally appropriate and contextually relevant.

Types of Documents We Translate

Our expertise covers a wide array of documents, including but not limited to:

  • Legal Documents: Contracts, Affidavits, Court Orders, Powers of Attorney, Judgments.
  • Personal Documents: Birth/Marriage/Death Certificates, Passports, Driver's Licenses, Diplomas.
  • Educational Documents: Degrees, Transcripts, Mark Sheets, Certificates.
  • Commercial Documents: Business Agreements, Certificates of Incorporation, Invoices, Financial Reports.
  • Technical Documents: Manuals, Patents, Reports.
  • Medical Documents: Reports, Records, Prescriptions.

This certification adds legal validity to your translated documents.
