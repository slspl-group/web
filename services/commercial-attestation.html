Commercial Document Attestation

Validating your business documents for international trade, expansion, and legal compliance.

What is Commercial Document Attestation?

Commercial Document Attestation is the process of authenticating business-related documents required for international operations. This includes documents like Certificates of Origin, Invoices, Packing Lists, Powers of Attorney, Board Resolutions, Agreements, and Certificates of Incorporation. Attestation confirms the document's legitimacy for use in foreign countries.

This process is vital for companies involved in international trade, setting up overseas branches, exporting goods, or engaging in cross-border legal matters.

Importance of Commercial Document Attestation

Attesting commercial documents is essential for:

  • Establishing legal presence or branches abroad.
  • Facilitating international trade and customs clearance.
  • Opening corporate bank accounts in foreign countries.
  • Participating in international tenders and contracts.
  • Resolving international business disputes.
  • Ensuring compliance with foreign regulations.

Common Commercial Documents Requiring Attestation

  • Certificate of Origin
  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing List
  • Memorandum & Articles of Association
  • Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
  • Board Resolution
  • Power of Attorney (Commercial)
  • Agency Agreements
  • Financial Statements

Our Attestation Process

We provide a streamlined process for commercial document attestation:

  1. Document Review & Consultation: We analyze your specific requirements and the documents involved.
  2. Chamber of Commerce Attestation: Initial verification by the relevant Chamber of Commerce.
  3. MEA Attestation / Apostille: Authentication by the Ministry of External Affairs.
  4. Embassy / Consulate Attestation: Final attestation by the embassy of the destination country.
  5. Secure Handling & Delivery: Careful management and return of your attested documents.
