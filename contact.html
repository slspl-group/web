FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does the attestation process take?

The attestation process typically takes between 7-15 working days, depending on the type of documents and the destination country. Express services are available for urgent requirements.

Do I need to visit your office to submit documents?

No, it's not mandatory. We offer pick-up services for your documents within Delhi NCR. For clients from other cities, documents can be sent via courier, and we'll process them remotely.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, UPI payments, credit/debit cards, and cash for in-person transactions. We provide proper invoices and receipts for all payments.

Do you provide translation services for documents in foreign languages?

Yes, we offer professional translation services for multiple languages. Our certified translators ensure accurate translations that meet international standards and requirements.
